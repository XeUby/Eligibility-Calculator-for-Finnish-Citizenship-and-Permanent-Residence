Application Requirements (March 2026)

Citizenship Requirements

  • Period of Residence: 5 years of continuous residence (with language skills) or 8 years (new law standard).
  • Language Skills: Satisfactory skills in Finnish or Swedish. Most common: YKI test (level 3/4).
  • Integrity: No criminal record, no restraining orders, and no unpaid taxes, fines, or maintenance payments.
  • Means of Support: You must provide a reliable report on your source of income.

PR Requirements

  • Period of Residence: 4 years of continuous residence with an A-permit.
  • Continuous Permit: Your current permit must be a continuous (A) permit.
  • Income: You must have sufficient income (salary, business income, or social benefits in some cases).
  • Automatic: PR does not expire as long as you stay in Finland, but the card must be renewed every 5 years.

💳 Fees & Processing (2026)

  • Citizenship Application: €460 (Electronic) / €690 (Paper).
  • Permanent Residence: €160 (Electronic) / €430 (Paper).
  • YKI Language Test: Approximately €176.
  • Processing Time: 6–18 months depending on queue.

⚠️ Important: Changes in Legislation

As of 2025-2026, the Finnish government has tightened the Citizenship Act. The required period for citizenship may be extended to 8 years for those without proven language skills or specific integration status. This calculator follows the 5-year model (standard for students-to-workers with YKI).

This calculator is an educational tool. For legal decisions, always refer to the official Finnish Immigration Service (Migri).
